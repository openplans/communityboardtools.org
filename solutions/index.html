Tools

How can technology empower Community Boards? Here are some ideas for simple tools to make the most of citizen engagment – to improve visibility, outreach, engagement, data collection, and more.

Each solution is a tool or method that we’ve heard Boards using, or an idea for a new tool. We give a few comments about each tool, plus links to other sources of info. For more in-depth listings of engagement tools, see our Next Steps reading list.

We know that many New Yorkers do not have access to the Internet, or do not use it as a source of information. Read more about our thoughts concering the digital divide here.
