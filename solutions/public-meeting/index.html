Public Meeting

Public monthly meetings held by all Community Boards. At these meetings, Board members address items of concern to the public.

Anyone can attend a Community Board meeting. Public meetings are an opportunity for residents to take an active part in local government. At the meetings, people canÂ hear about what’s happening in their neighborhood, voice their opinion on projects, and bring up topics for discussion.
